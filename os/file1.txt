Deep within the blood of every Saiyan lies the **Zenaki Boost**, a genetic cheat code that ensures whatever doesn't kill them makes them exponentially stronger. It’s a biological drive toward evolution that makes them one of the most feared warrior races in the multiverse.

### The Biology of a Warrior

Unlike most species, Saiyans are born with a fixed hairstyle that never changes after birth (unless they’re a hybrid). Their aging process is also uniquely adapted for combat; they remain in their physical prime until about age 80 to ensure they can fight for as long as possible before a rapid decline.

### Social Hierarchy

The Saiyan social structure was brutally simple, based entirely on **Battle Power**:

* **Low-Class Warriors:** The majority of the population (e.g., Bardock, Raditz).
* **Mid-Class:** A rare group of about 10 elite soldiers.
* **Elite-Class:** Historically reserved only for King Vegeta and Prince Vegeta.

### The Legend and the Loss

While the Great Ape transformation (Oozaru) allowed them to conquer planets under the full moon, it was the myth of the **Super Saiyan** that truly terrified the tyrant Frieza. He feared the day a Saiyan would surpass their natural limits—a fear that eventually led to the destruction of Planet Vegeta.
